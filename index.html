About

Do you enjoy beer? So do we. That is why we started Be.er

At Be.er we believe that no one should ever have to feel overwhelmed by all of the beer choices available to them. Just reach out to us and answer a few simple questions, and we'll recommend a beer for you. We'll even try to locate it for you in your area.

Are you at a store? Reach out to us and let us know what beers you are trying to decide between, and we'll help you decide. We like you, and beer that much.
